WPS Office Complete Removal Guide

This guide outlines the steps to fully uninstall WPS Office and remove user-specific data to ensure a clean reinstall.

1. Uninstall WPS Office

  1. Go to Settings > Apps and uninstall all WPS Office components.
  2. Alternatively, use appwiz.cpl (Run dialog) to open Programs & Features and uninstall from there.

2. Delete User Data Folders

Delete the following folders for the affected user (replace <username> with the actual username):

C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Local\Kingsoft
C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Roaming\Kingsoft
C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Local\WPS Office
C:\Users\<username>\AppData\Roaming\WPS Office

3. Clean Up Registry

  1. Open regedit (Windows + R â†’ type regedit).
  2. Navigate to the following registry paths and delete WPS or Kingsoft-related entries under the user's SID:
    • HKEY_USERS\S-1-5-21-...\Software\Kingsoft
    • HKEY_USERS\S-1-5-21-...\Software\WPSOffice
  3. Do not delete unrelated keys. Proceed with caution.

4. Reboot & Test

After deletion, reboot the system.
